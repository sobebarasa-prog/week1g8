Role Play in Front of the Class
👥 10 PAIRS perform

Each pair gets a scenario below. Practise with your partner, then perform your telephone conversation in front of the class. Use polite words throughout. Record your voices to listen back.

💡
Before You Perform
  • 👋Greet and introduce yourself when the call connects.
  • 🗣Speak clearly and steadily — not too fast or too quietly.
  • 🙏Use: please, thank you, may I, certainly, of course.
  • 👂Listen fully before responding — do not interrupt.
  • 📞End politely: Goodbye, take care, thank you for your time.
